FAQ

Common questions

Is my health data private and secure?
Your health data belongs to you. We never sell it, never share it with advertisers, and you have full control over what's stored and how it's used. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
What devices and platforms does PeakRoutine support?
PeakRoutine is currently available on iOS via TestFlight, with deep Apple Health integration. The App Store launch is coming soon. Android support is actively being developed.
How do I access the app after purchase?
After your purchase is confirmed, you'll receive an email with your TestFlight download link and onboarding instructions. The whole process takes less than 2 minutes.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from the app or through the App Store. There are no cancellation fees or complicated processes.
What's the difference between Pro and Elite?
Pro gives you all the core AI health analytics, personalized insights, habit tracking, and gamification features. Elite adds access to specialized AI coaches (Sleep, Fitness, Nutrition, Recovery) who analyze your personal data and give tailored recommendations in real-time — up to 20 messages per day.
Do I need to manually enter my health data?
Almost never. PeakRoutine automatically syncs sleep, HRV, steps, heart rate, and calorie data from Apple Health with one-time permission. The only things you manually log are mood, stress, nutrition, and journaling entries — which take less than 2 minutes per day.
