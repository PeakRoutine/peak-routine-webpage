What is PeakRoutine?

PeakRoutine is an AI-powered health platform that automatically pulls data from Apple Health — sleep, HRV, steps, heart rate, and more — then surfaces personalized insights and builds sustainable habits across every dimension of your well-being. With built-in AI coaches, you get deeper recommendations based on your own data, so every decision is backed by what your body is actually telling you.

Apple Health Auto-Sync

Automatically pulls sleep, HRV, steps, resting heart rate, and calories from Apple Health. No manual entry — your data is always up to date.

Nutrition, Hydration & Caffeine

Log meals from a database of millions of foods, track macros, and monitor hydration and caffeine intake — all with personalized timing recommendations.

AI-Powered Pattern Insights

Discover correlations like "Your HRV improves when you sleep before 11PM" — with confidence scores. Actionable intelligence, not just raw data.

Mental Health & Journaling

Track mood, stress levels, and daily reflections. Guided breathing exercises and meditation videos help you manage stress before it manages you.

Streaks, Badges & Habit Milestones

Build habits with milestone targets at 7, 14, 30, 90, and 365 days. Earn badges, level up, and celebrate every win — consistency has never been this motivating.

Personalized Health Plans

A 5-step lifestyle questionnaire on day one — exercise habits, sleep schedule, sunlight, and nutrition — then AI builds a plan that truly fits your life.

AI Health Coaches

Chat with specialized AI coaches for sleep, nutrition, exercise, and stress. They analyze your actual health data and give personalized recommendations — like having a team of experts in your pocket.

Simple to Start

Up and Running in 3 Steps

No complicated setup. PeakRoutine gets to know you, connects to your data, and starts working on day one.

Step 1

Tell us about your lifestyle

A quick 5-step questionnaire covers your exercise habits, sleep schedule, wake time, sunlight exposure, and dinner timing. Your personalized baseline is ready immediately.

Step 2

Connect Apple Health

Grant one-time permission and PeakRoutine automatically syncs your sleep, HRV, steps, resting heart rate, and calorie data. No manual logging needed — ever.

Step 3

Get your personalized plan

AI analyzes your patterns and surfaces insights like "Your recovery improves 23% when you sleep before 11PM." Daily health scores and habit recommendations update as you grow.
